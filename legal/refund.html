Refund Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

⚠️ Please read this policy carefully before investing. All investments on this platform are subject to locking periods tied to active ROI plans.

1. General Policy

All investments made on Indo Global Services are committed to active investment plans. Once an investment is confirmed and activated, it enters a 400-day ROI cycle and cannot be withdrawn as a lump sum. Returns are paid out progressively on a daily basis.

2. Withdrawal of Earnings

Daily earned ROI accumulates in your wallet and is available for withdrawal subject to:

  • Minimum withdrawal of $10 USD
  • A 5% service charge deducted from the withdrawal amount
  • Email OTP verification for security
  • Processing time: 1–5 business days after admin approval

3. Erroneous Transactions

If you believe a deposit was made in error (e.g. technical double-charge), contact us within 48 hours of the transaction with proof. We will investigate and refund confirmed erroneous charges within 10 business days.

4. Fraud or Unauthorized Access

If your account was accessed without authorization, you must notify us immediately. We will investigate and, if fraud is confirmed, may restore wallet balances to their pre-incident state.

5. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Voluntary investment decisions
  • Earnings lost due to tier cap enforcement
  • The 5% service charge on processed withdrawals
  • Accounts suspended for violations of the Terms & Conditions

6. Contact Us

To submit a refund claim: support@indoglobalservices.in
