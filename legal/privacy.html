Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Indo Global Services ("we", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password (stored as encrypted hash)
  • Financial Information: Investment amounts, wallet balances, transaction records
  • Identity Verification (KYC): Government ID documents when required by law
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information for security purposes
  • Google OAuth: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email from Google

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your investment account
  • To calculate and distribute ROI, referral commissions, and bonuses
  • To send withdrawal OTP codes and account notifications via email
  • To detect fraud and ensure platform security
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on a secure VPS server encrypted at rest. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text. We use HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Stripe (payment processor) — for processing deposits
  • Google (if you use Google sign-in)
  • As required by applicable law or government authority

5. Cookies

We use session cookies for authentication purposes only. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

7. Contact

Privacy enquiries: privacy@indoglobalservices.in
